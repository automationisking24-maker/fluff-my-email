# Fluff My Email – Privacy Policy

Fluff My Email is a Chrome extension that helps users rewrite and improve the tone of their emails using AI.

## Data Handling

- The extension stores user preferences and API keys locally in the user’s browser using `chrome.storage`.
- Email text entered by the user is sent only to the AI provider selected by the user (OpenAI, Google Gemini, or Anthropic) for the sole purpose of rewriting the email.
- No user data is collected, stored, or transmitted to the developer.
- No data is shared with third parties beyond the selected AI provider.

## Data Usage

- The extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or cookies.
- The extension does not sell, rent, or trade user data.
